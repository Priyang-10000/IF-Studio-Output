Future Outlook
Projected Coal Phase-down Milestones
2024–2030
EU coal phase-out accelerates; 40+ countries committed to no new coal power. Renewables to displace ~800 GW of coal capacity.
2030
IEA Net Zero: No new coal mines or plants approved. Global coal demand falls 55% vs 2020 levels in 1.5°C scenarios.
2040
Advanced economies fully phase out unabated coal power. CCUS technology still nascent for remaining coal use.
2050
Net-zero target. Coal use limited to CCUS-equipped plants. Coking coal remains for specialty steelmaking.
POWERING THE TRANSITION
Despite decline in power generation, coal remains critical for metallurgical processes. COP28 (2023) pledged "transitioning away" from fossil fuels.
