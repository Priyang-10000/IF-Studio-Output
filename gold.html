Gold Through History
Key milestones in humanity's relationship with gold
~4600 BCE
Oldest gold artifacts found in Varna Necropolis, Bulgaria — earliest known worked gold.
~560 BCE
First pure gold coins minted under King Croesus of Lydia (modern Turkey).
1848
California Gold Rush begins at Sutter's Mill, triggering mass migration of 300,000+ people.
1900
U.S. Gold Standard Act passed — $20.67/oz fixed price established.
1944
Bretton Woods Agreement pegs global currencies to USD at $35/troy oz.
1971
Nixon Shock — U.S. ends gold convertibility, launching the fiat era and gold's free float.
2004
World's first gold ETF (SPDR Gold Shares) launches on NYSE; democratizes gold investing.
2025
Gold hits all-time high of ~$3,100/oz amid geopolitical tensions and de-dollarization.
