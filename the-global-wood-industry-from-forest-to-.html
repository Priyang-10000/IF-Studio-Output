Mass Timber Rising
Cross-laminated timber (CLT) is projected to replace steel in mid-rise buildings by 2030, reducing construction emissions by up to 75%.
Digital Marketplace
Online wood & lumber trading platforms grew 42% post-pandemic, with B2B digital sales expected to reach $18B by 2027.
Bioenergy Demand
Wood pellet production has tripled since 2010, reaching 36 million tonnes in 2023, driven by EU renewable energy mandates.
Net Zero Forests
145 nations pledged to end deforestation by 2030 at COP26. Managed forests currently sequester 2.6 billion tonnes of CO₂ annually.
